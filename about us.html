About Us

Story Behind Our Company

JJ ENERGY is a 100% Black owned petroleum distribution company that plays in the energy space of Africa. We are a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor. You can be rest assured that all products supplied to clients by JJ ENERGY is of the highest quality, best specification and arrives timeously. JJ ENERGY’s key strength is management of its own delivery fleet which allows timeous delivery to customers and allows close management of customers’ stock levels.This also allows customers to have 24 hour turn-around from order to delivery and on occasion emergency orders can be serviced as far as possible. We can also setup storage facilities on our client’s premises. JJ ENERGY can supply commercial and industrial based customers as well as the agricultural, mining, marine and aviation industry. We value professionalism in all we do. The company focuses on small, medium and big customers in South Africa and Export Markets.


Our sevices

• We offer a wide range of petroleum products (petrol unleaded, diesel, paraffin)
• We manufacture and supply clients with tanks and pumps.
• We supply clients with a range of oils and lubricants for their fleet.
• We offer our clients with logistic services.
• We offer free maintenance on clients’ fuel equipment
